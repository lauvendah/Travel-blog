Nabuyole falls in Bungoma County.

These waterfalls (Nabuyole falls) are close to Webuye, a rural town in Bungoma county that has definitely a lot of challenges and poverty, but also amazing landscapes and very helpful, smart people. There is not really tourism around, even this location is described as one of the sightseeing highlights in Bungoma county. It is a very natural place. And in the months after rainy season it is definitely worth to go.
